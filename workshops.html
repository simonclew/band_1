Workshops and Teaching

Tony has been teaching private lessons, group workshops and masterclasses for over 20 years. He loves sharing his passion and skills with people from all over the world. Tony is frequently teaching at universities, such as the University of Limerick, Berklee College of Music, Newcastle University, Royal Scottish Conservatoire, City University of New York; as well as camps like Steve Kaufman’s or Alasdair Fraser’s. For the last 11 years he has been hosting his very own annual guitar weekend in the beautiful town of Elora, Ontario. These weekends host about fifteen guitarists eager to learn, play and spend time together in a supportive environment. Just recently Tony added another location to the list and started another guitar weekend in California. Come check it out!

Contact Tony for private guitar lessons for $80/hour., in person or via Skype.
